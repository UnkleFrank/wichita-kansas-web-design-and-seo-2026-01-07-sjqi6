Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web design, SEO, and hosting services.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term investment, and results typically become visible within 3-6 months, depending on your industry and competition level. Some improvements, like better click-through rates from optimized titles and meta descriptions, can happen immediately. We focus on sustainable, organic growth that compounds over time. Most of our clients see significant improvements in rankings and traffic within the first six months, with continued growth as we build authority and expand our keyword targeting.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely! We specialize in website redesigns for businesses that want to modernize their online presence. We'll assess your current site, preserve any valuable content and SEO equity, and rebuild it with modern design principles, better user experience, and improved performance. We can also migrate your domain and maintain your search rankings throughout the process. Many of our redesign clients see improved conversion rates and user engagement immediately after launch.

What's included in your hosting packages?

Our hosting packages include 99.9% uptime guarantee, unlimited bandwidth, automatic daily backups, SSL certificates for security, email hosting, one-click WordPress installation, and 24/7 technical support. We also provide performance optimization, security monitoring, and automatic software updates. All packages come with a 30-day money-back guarantee, so you can try our service risk-free. We handle all the technical aspects so you can focus on your business.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive website maintenance packages that include security updates, plugin updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and regular backups. We can handle everything from fixing broken links to adding new pages and features. Our maintenance plans are flexible and can be customized to your specific needs. Most clients appreciate having a dedicated team handle their website so they don't have to worry about technical issues or security vulnerabilities.

How do you measure the success of a web design or SEO project?

We measure success through specific, measurable metrics that align with your business goals. For web design, we track metrics like conversion rates, bounce rates, time on page, and user engagement. For SEO, we monitor keyword rankings, organic traffic, click-through rates, and lead generation. We provide detailed monthly reports showing exactly how your website and SEO campaigns are performing and what actions we're taking to improve results. Transparency and accountability are core to our approach.
