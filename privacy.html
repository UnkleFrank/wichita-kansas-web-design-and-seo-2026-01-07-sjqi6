Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and enhance your browsing experience. Cookies may be "persistent" (remaining on your device until you delete them) or "session-based" (deleted when you close your browser).

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality, security, and service delivery.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website and identify areas for improvement.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity to deliver targeted advertising and marketing messages.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers (such as Google Analytics, advertising networks, and social media platforms) to place cookies on your device. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their cookie use. You can control third-party cookies through your browser settings and opt-out mechanisms provided by these services.

Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Delete existing cookies from your device

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to access certain features. For more information about managing cookies, visit your browser's help section or www.allaboutcookies.org.

Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Our website currently does not respond to Do Not Track signals due to the lack of industry-wide standards. However, you can use other tools to control data collection and use as described in this Privacy Policy.
